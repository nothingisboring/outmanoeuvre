How to Play Conceits

Conceits is a cultural puzzle game that offers eccentric clues ("conceits")to find a hidden novel, poem, song, film, or other famous cultural work.

Game Rules:

  1. You'll be presented with 5 eccentically cryptic clues, one at a time, decreasing in difficulty.
  2. Solve each clue to reveal a word or phrase linked to the cultural work.
  3. After solving each clue, you'll have a chance to guess the composition.
  4. You have a total of 5 attempts across all clues.
  5. Puzzles get harder from Monday-Friday. At the weekend, they get nuts.
  6. Earn 10 points for each solved clue and bonus points for guessing the famous work early.

Tips for Solving the "Conceits":

  • Clues may be straight-up literal or cryptic with a wordplay component.
  • The definition is not always included - naughty, I know.
  • Common types of wordplay include homophones, stage directions, film quotes, grammar references - but you won't need all those cryptic abbreviations!
  • Numbers in parentheses indicate the length of the answer.
  • You should get used to the eccentric clueing style over time!
